## SEO Strategy

SEO is a primary business goal — Mustafa runs paid ads and needs strong organic ranking in a competitive DFW market.

**Target market:** Dallas-Fort Worth metro — high competition for "coffee catering DFW", "wedding coffee bar Dallas", "corporate coffee catering Dallas", etc.

**SEO priorities:**
- City/location landing pages (already have 9 DFW cities — Allen, McKinney, Plano, Frisco, Richardson, Southlake, Fort Worth, Irving, Arlington)
- Service-specific pages (wedding, corporate, brand activation) with keyword-rich copy
- Structured data / schema markup (LocalBusiness, Event, FAQPage, Review)
- Fast page load (Core Web Vitals — big ranking factor)
- Mobile-first design (Google mobile-first indexing)
- Meta titles and descriptions on every page
- Open Graph tags for social sharing
- Sitemap.xml and robots.txt
- Google Business Profile alignment (NAP consistency: Name, Address, Phone)
- Blog/content section for long-tail keywords ("how to hire a coffee cart for a wedding in Dallas", etc.)
- Internal linking between location pages and service pages

**Competitive keywords to target:**
- "coffee cart catering Dallas"
- "wedding coffee bar DFW"
- "corporate coffee catering Dallas"
- "mobile espresso bar Dallas"
- "coffee catering [city name]" for each of the 9 DFW cities
